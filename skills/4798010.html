The Guardian Of The Flame Wind

Skill · Skills #4798010

Description

The Guardian of the Flame Wind Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 90 Range: 9 Bell and Ais jointly cast a defensive skill, using the power of wind to Protect Party and enhance their speed. Ais increases the Move Spd of herself and all Teammates within a 9-meter radius by 10% and their Flee by 50. Meanwhile, Bell provides a damage Absorb Shield to Teammates within the area, with the Absorb value equal to his max HP * 50%, lasting for 30 seconds. If Ais is not in the team, only the damage Absorb Shield will be provided. The Shields and buffs provided by Bell and Ais can stack. Lvl: 9
