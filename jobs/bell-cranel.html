A

Argonaut

Lvl: 1LeadSkill
Guide to gain energy, when the energy progress reaches 100%%, you will acquire the 60-second [Argonaut] state: transforming skills into [Limit Off]. During guidance, the energy progress increases by +10%% per second, and using [Fire Bolt] also increases the energy progress by +10%%. The guidance state can be interrupted at any time, and the energy progress will be preserved. When using [Limit Off], your Move Spd increases to the limit, each Auto Attack deals an additional attack, the [Rapid Escape] skill effect is raised to 100%%, lasting for 10 seconds. After the duration ends, the [Argonaut] state is lifted. [Limit Off] has a fixed Cooldown Time of 30 seconds which cannot be reduced.
